Crowd filtering for Japan Earthquake

How it works: Real simple. You watch the tweets on the left, which are a sample from the live stream. If a tweet matches one of the categories (Information about damage, Advice or a Request), hit the button and it'll appear in the relevant column. The purpose is to help people doing reports or information tracking get a filtered feed so they can do their job faster.

If you have any questions about this service, richard@redspider.co.nz or call 021 478219. This service only currently works using Google Chrome. Sorry
